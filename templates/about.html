About EngiStore

EngiStore is an intelligent inventory system built for engineers, by engineers.

Designed for factory maintenance teams, pharmaceutical plants, and industrial technicians, it replaces clunky spreadsheets and guesswork with real-time tracking, automated alerts, and seamless part-to-machine logging—so your team spends less time searching for parts and more time keeping operations running.

How EngiStore Simplifies Your Workflow:
  • 📦 Smarter Stock Control
    Browse parts by category, location, or machine—with color-coded warnings for low stock.
  • 🛠️ Maintenance Made Traceable
    Every part used is logged to its asset, creating a repair history that's auditable in seconds.
  • 📉 Automated Inventory Awareness
    Get visual cues for low stock parts before they impact operations.
  • 👥 Role-Smart Access
    Admins get full control, technicians log usage, and viewers check stock—no more unauthorized withdrawals.
  • 📩 Missing Something?
    Request new items directly in the system, with approvals routed to managers.
Built for Reliability
  • Backend: Django + Python (battle-tested for heavy industry use)
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5 (clean, works even on factory-floor tablets)
  • Database: SQLite (dev) / PostgreSQL-ready (scales when you do)
  • Deployment: Render (free hosting with production-ready options)
  • Version Control: Git & GitHub (full change history)
Security & Access
  • 🔐 Role-based permissions
    Admin, Manager, Technician, Viewer—each with tailored access
  • 📊 Admin dashboard
    Quick stats and complete request visibility
  • 📥 Full audit trails
    Every part usage logged with user tracking and timestamps
  • 🔄 Sandbox safety
    Demo accounts reset every 24 hours
